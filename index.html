Step 1 — Sensor Calibration

Attach the calibration cap to the sensor end. Enter the known internal length of the cap, then click Calibrate. This corrects for any sensor offset.

Step 2 — Ice Thickness

Remove the calibration cap. Measure ice thickness with a tape through the auger hole and enter it below. Expected initial freeboard = ice thickness × 8.3%.

Step 3 — Install & Verify

Install the sensor through the ice with the flange seated flush on the surface. Then click Verify to compare the measured freeboard against the expected value.
